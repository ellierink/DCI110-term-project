EXAMINING FACTORS INFLUENCING PROPERTY PRICES IN LEXINGTON
INTR-202

Lexington's population owns many farms and large plots of land. We were interested to see how property prices in Lexington are influenced by acreage and whether or not a property has a house on it. We used R to create a multiple regression model that showed us when a property size increases by 1 acre, its price increases by $7,170, on average, if all other factors are fixed, and having a house on a property increases the price of a property by $344,056, on average, if all other factors are fixed.
